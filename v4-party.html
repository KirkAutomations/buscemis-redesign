Our Story

Three Generations of Great Italian Taste

In 1955, Paul A. Buscemi opened a small party store in Detroit with big dreams. He introduced the Italian submarine sandwich to the Detroit area, creating the now-trademarked Torpedo and Baby Sub. After years perfecting his pizza recipe, he and son Anthony franchised the concept in 1974. Today, 50+ locations serve Michigan with the same quality and family pride.
