Our Story

Three Generations of Great Italian Taste

In 1955, Paul A. Buscemi acquired a small party store in Detroit with little capital but relentless determination. He introduced the Italian submarine sandwich to the Detroit area, creating the now-trademarked Torpedo and Baby Sub. After years of perfecting his dough, sauce, and recipes, he crafted what we still believe is the finest Italian pizza in the market. When son Anthony joined the family business, they saw an opportunity. In 1974, they became the first company to franchise the Party Shoppe concept in Michigan. Today, with over 50 locations across the state, the Buscemi name stands for quality, tradition, and that unmistakable Detroit flavor.

FAQ

Common Questions

What is Detroit-style pizza?
Detroit-style pizza is baked in a square steel pan, producing thick, airy dough with a crispy, caramelized cheese edge. Our recipe was perfected by Paul Buscemi himself over years of experimentation with dough, sauce, and ingredients. The result is a pizza with a golden, crunchy crust and cheese that runs all the way to the edge. Trust the crust.
Can I order online?
Yes. Many of our locations offer online ordering through our platform. Check if your local Buscemi's participates and place your order for pickup or delivery right from your phone or computer.
Do you offer catering?
Absolutely. We offer party paks, family meal deals, quarter-tray and full-tray sizes perfect for graduations, corporate events, game days, and any gathering. Contact your local Buscemi's for catering options and pricing.
How do I find my nearest location?
Visit our Locations page and enter your zip code or scroll through our complete list of 50+ Michigan locations. Each listing includes the address and phone number so you can call ahead.
What's a Torpedo sub?
The Torpedo is our signature Italian submarine sandwich, trademarked and served since the 1950s. Paul Buscemi was the first to bring the Italian sub to the Detroit area. It's a full-sized sub loaded with Italian meats, cheese, and fresh toppings. The Baby Sub is the smaller version. Both are available hot or cold.
How do I franchise with Buscemi's?
Visit our Franchise page or contact us directly. We've been franchising since 1974 and offer qualified buyers a proven concept with better buying power, marketing support, and a 70+ year reputation. Buscemi's is the place for that GREAT ITALIAN TASTE.
